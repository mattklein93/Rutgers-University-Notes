How to Convert a Number to it's Complement
  • Given a number, 23, how to convert to binary:
    • $$$2^0 2^1 2^2 2^3 2^4 …$$$ pick largest, but not over the number. That is our total size, so here will be $$$10000$$$. Continue to go down, changing where the 1 is based on the exponent. See here.
  • Converting One's Complement to -23 is to:
    1. Convert each bit
    2. Add one
    3. $$$11101001$$$
  • What about going back to 23?
    • This does not account for overflow where max is $$$2^{31}-1$$$. The minimum is $$$(-2^{31}-1)-1$$$
